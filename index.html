About Us

The UAE's Trusted End-to-End Interior Design & Fit-Out Partner

Smart Design. Modern Interiors. Curated to Fit Your Budget.

GurAmar Decor & Interiors is a full-service interior design and fit-out company with offices in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, serving clients all over the UAE. We partner with homeowners, developers, and business owners to design and build spaces that are beautiful, functional, and true to their vision.

Our integrated team manages everything under one roof — from initial design concepts and 3D renders through to civil works, MEP services, custom furniture, kitchen and bathroom renovations, and final finishing. One team, one point of contact, zero hassle — and a consistent commitment to quality at every stage.

We believe exceptional design should be accessible. With transparent pricing, smart material choices, and meticulous project management, we deliver finishes that look high-end without the inflated cost.
